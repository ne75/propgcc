\subsection{Detailed Description}
Defines error condition reporting macros. 

These simple macros support error conditions for the libraries. The only macros required by A\+N\+S\+I-\/C are E\+D\+OM, E\+I\+L\+S\+EQ, and E\+R\+A\+N\+GE.

The undocumented \char`\"{}\+Unlikely\char`\"{} and \char`\"{}\+Network\char`\"{} macros are included for compatibility with G\+NU libstdc++.
